## What it does

- Detects L2 operational anomalies in real time (sync, sequencer, infra)
- Generates policy-based recovery plans
- Executes low-risk actions automatically
- Requests approval for high-risk actions (ChatOps)
- Verifies outcomes and preserves audit trails

---

## Why now

- L2/Rollup operations are getting more complex
- Manual incident handling increases MTTR and operator burden
- Teams need safe automation with governance, not black-box autopilot

---

## How it works

1. **Observe** — metrics, logs, and RPC signals
2. **Decide** — incident analysis + risk-scored action plan
3. **Act** — automated or approval-gated execution, then verification

---

## Deployment Options

### Local Container (Docker Compose)
- Fast 10-minute local setup
- Best for evaluation and development

### Kubernetes (AWS EKS)
- Production-grade deployment path
- Scales with team and infra complexity

---

## Safety & Control

- Risk-tiered action policy (low/medium/high/critical)
- Forbidden actions by default for destructive operations
- High-risk actions require explicit approval
- Full audit history for every decision and action
